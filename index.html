Biography

I'm a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Department of Computer Science of UMass, Amherst, working with Professor Don Towsley and Mohammad Hajiesmaili. I got my PhD degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2018, (fortunately) supervised by Professor Wing Shing Wong. Prior to that, I received my BEng and Msc degree in 2012 and 2015, respectively, both from University of Science and Technology of China.

Research Interests

  • Networking

    • Software Defined Networking, Cloud Computing, Data Centers, etc. 
    • Performance Analysis, Modeling, and Online Resource Optimization for Networked Computing Systems 

  • Learning Theory  

    • Expert Problems: Lipschitz Expert, Heterogeneous Expert, Multi-scale Online Learing
    • Multi-Armed Bandits (MABs): Stochastic Bandits, Nonstochastic Bandits, Distributed Bandits and Bandits with Attacks/Corruptions
    • Online Convex Optimization (OCO)

  • Online Optimization

    • Online Knapsack Problems: Online Multidimensional Knapsack, Online Multiple Knapsack, Online Knapsack with Departure etc.
    • Online Linear Programming, Online Buffer Management
